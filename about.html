About Me

A story shaped by movement, service, and curiosity

I am building a career at the intersection of people, strategy, and technology. Everything I have done so far contributes to that direction.

My Story

I did not leave my past behind. I am building on it.

From Colombia to the United States

Moving from Colombia to the United States meant learning a new language, adapting to a different culture, and building a new life from the ground up. That experience taught me resilience, humility, and how to keep learning even when the path is unfamiliar.

Marketing built my strategic foundation

My background in marketing and brand management gave me a strong understanding of positioning, communication, audience needs, and business goals. I learned how to connect ideas to outcomes and how to think beyond tactics.

Healthcare made my work more human

Working as a Patient Navigator and Prevention Specialist deepened my empathy. I supported people facing complex systems, high-stakes decisions, and everyday barriers to care. That work taught me to communicate clearly, stay calm, and focus on practical solutions.

AI and development are the next chapter

I am now learning AI and software development because I want to build products that improve people's lives. Rather than leaving marketing and healthcare behind, I am combining both with technology to solve human problems in smarter and more scalable ways.
